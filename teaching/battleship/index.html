Welcome to Battleship!

Your mission is to sink all three battleships, all of size three length, scattered in the vast ocean grid!

Enter your guess into the form cell (capital letter then number ex. G6) then click fire to launch your attack. The called grid location will show a miss or a hit graphic, along with a corresponding message at the bottom of the board. You will be commended for your success once you sink all three battleships!

Good luck!
